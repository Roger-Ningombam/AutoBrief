Privacy Policy & Website Details

How AutoBrief Works

AutoBrief uses advanced Large Language Models powered by high-performance Groq API to analyze and summarize books. Our system focuses on understanding meaning and key arguments—not just keywords—so summaries stay true to the author’s intent.

Your Privacy Comes First

  • No storage: Your searches and inputs are processed in real time and immediately discarded.
  • No training on your data: We do not store, reuse, or train our models on your personal queries.
  • No tracking: AutoBrief is cookie-free and does not use ads or third-party trackers.

Accuracy & Similar Book Titles

Many books share the same title. For best results, we recommend searching using “Title by Author.” If only a title is provided, AutoBrief defaults to the most well-known version—but adding the author ensures accuracy.

AI Limitations

AutoBrief summaries are designed as study aids, not replacements for reading the full book. While we strive for accuracy, occasional errors can occur—please verify important details with the original text.

Usage Terms

AutoBrief is for personal and educational use only. Generated summaries may not be resold or presented as the original author’s work.
